FAQs

What should I wear?

For our beloved guests, we're keeping it semi formal, but dressy. No need for suits and ball gowns, but please follow the attire guidelines mentioned above and please do not wear jeans, mini skirt, shorts, shirts, slippers, sneakers or any other casual attire. If you have specific questions, please feel free to reach out.

What time should we arrive at the church?

We humbly request for you to arrive at the specified venue atleast 30 mins prior the appointed time so you can comfortably settle in.

When is the RSVP deadline?

To help us finalize our wedding plans, please RSVP by August 01, 2025 for an accurate headcount. Anyone who has not responded by this date will unfortunately not be included to the final guest list.

Will there be parking available at the venues?

Yes, both the church and reception venues have ample parking space available for all guests.

Can we bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate the guests listed on the invitation. Please understand that we cannot accept plus ones unless specifically indicated on your invitation.

Are children welcome at the wedding?

While we love children, we have decided to keep our special day an adults-only celebration. Children of the immediate family are the only exceptions. We hope this advance notice allows you to make appropriate arrangements.

Will there be transportation between venues?

We will provide shuttle service between the church and reception venue.

What time will the reception end?

The reception is scheduled to end at 5:00 PM. We look forward to celebrating with you throughout the afternoon!

Are we expected to bring a gift?

As love is what makes it all about, your presence and prayers are all that we request. But should you still believe that a gift is worth giving, a cash gift/gift card to help us with our future is warmly appreciated.
